About Us

Mission Statement

The Quince Orchard High School Engineering & Robotics Club hopes to challenge the students of Quince Orchard High School to creatively apply STEM principles to the completion of several projects including the FIRST® Tech Challenge (FTC), 3D printing, and drone builds.

Meetings

During the robotics season (September-January; through April if we qualify for states) we meet after-school. Outside of the robotics season we meet during lunch. See the calendar for additional information.

Outreach

Click here or email qorobotics@gmail.com to contact us about outreach opportunities. In the past we have presented at science fairs, scout meetings, and elementary schools.
