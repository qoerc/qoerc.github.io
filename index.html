About Us

Mission Statement

The Quince Orchard High School Engineering & Robotics Club hopes to challenge the students of Quince Orchard High School to creatively apply STEM principles to the completion of several projects including the FIRSTÂ® Tech Challenge (FTC), 3D printing, and drone builds.

Meetings

During the robotics season (September-January; through April if we qualify for states) we meet after-school. Outside of the robotics season we meet during lunch. See the calendar for additional information.

Outreach

Click here or email qorobotics@gmail.com to contact us about outreach opportunities. In the past we have presented at science fairs, scout meetings, and elementary schools.

FIRST Tech Challenge

We are FIRST Tech Challenge Team 7518 CouGears and Team 7519 CougarBotics from Quince Orchard High School in Gaithersburg, MD. Our two teams have been competing in FTC since the 2013-2014 season.
